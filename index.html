My "career" so far

I completed my Abitur in 2021, with the german grade of 1.6, after which I started my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at TU Berlin. During my Bachelor's, I spent one year working part-time each as a systems administrator for Cronon GmbH and a software developer for Codewerk GmbH. Through these jobs, I gained notable insights into how professional software is developed and how large software systems are operated. I finished my Bachelor's in 2025, with the german grade of 1.6, writing my thesis on attacking the RPMC security feature of modern SPI flash chips, through voltage-glitching and other hardware attacks.
